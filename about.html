Bahamut Night

Photography

I'm Gary, a photographer from Montreal, Quebec. Thank you for taking a look at my portfolio!

I started doing cosplay photography in 2011. I take hall shots, gathering photos and I do private shoots inside & outside of conventions.

Over the past few years, I've been to conventions in Canada and the US such as Otakuthon, Montreal Comiccon, Anime Boston, Anime North, G-Anime, Katsucon, Anime Central, Ottawa Comiccon, AnimeNext, Youmacon, MAGFest and Anime Expo.

As for the title "Bahamut Night Photographery", the Bahamut is the king of dragons, and one of my favorite Final Fantasy characters. I liked the name "Bahamut Knight", though decided on "Night" instead.

Please feel free to shoot me an email or message me on my other sites, found in my contact page, and I'll do my best to reply as soon as possible. I hope to see you around!
